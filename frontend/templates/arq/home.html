About Stop-and-Wait ARQ
How it works
  • 1 Sender transmits one packet and starts a timer.
  • 2 Receiver sends ACK on successful receipt.
  • 3 If timer expires before ACK → retransmit.
  • 4 After max retries without ACK → packet fails.
  • 5 Sender waits for ACK before sending next packet.
Event types
SEND — Packet transmitted
ACK — Acknowledged
DROP — Simulated loss
TIMEOUT — No ACK received
RETRANSMIT — Resending
SUCCESS — Delivered
